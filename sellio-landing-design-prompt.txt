WHAT SELLIO DOES (use this for copy)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

The problem it solves:
  • 70% of customers lose physical stamp/loyalty cards
  • Existing SaaS solutions charge 30%+ commission or per-customer fees
  • Building their own software is out of reach for small businesses
  • Most loyalty apps require the customer to download a native app (they don't)

The solution:
  • The business creates a digital loyalty program in 2 minutes
  • They get a unique QR code to show at the register
  • The customer scans it with their phone camera — no app download needed
  • They enter their phone number, get a digital stamp card
  • Stamps accumulate. When complete → reward is unlocked
  • The business sees all customers, stamps, and redemptions in a dashboard

Key differentiators:
  • Zero friction for the customer (just a browser, no download)
  • Ready in 2 minutes (not 2 days)
  • Free to start (no credit card)
  • Made for LATAM pricing and reality
  • No commission on rewards — flat subscription only

VISUAL DESIGN RULES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

- Backgrounds: alternating dark (stone-950) and light (stone-50) sections
- Gold is used SPARINGLY as accent — not everywhere, or loses impact
- Buttons with gold: text must be DARK (#0C0A09), not white
- Hero phone mockup: dramatic perspective tilt (10-15°), floating
- Section transitions: smooth gradient blends, not hard cuts
- Cards: subtle border with gold tint (border: 1px solid rgba(245,158,11,0.15))
- Shadows: warm-tinted (rgba(245,158,11,0.1) not cold gray)
- Icons: line-style SVGs in amber-400/500, 24-28px
- Spacing: generous — this is premium, not cramped
- Animations: fade-up on scroll, phone float, gold shimmer on headline
